ABOUT THE CAFE

One of the most food-focused animal cafes in Philippines serves light dishes like galettes and curries, with beer, wine and cocktails supplementing the usual coffee and tea. Before 6pm there's a one-beverage minimum order in lieu of a cover charge, and coffee and soft drinks start at 75 pesos. On weekends during the day you can pay for a three-hour session, which includes drinks. The atmosphere is more subdued than average - the animals seem to enjoy chlling out in the cardboard boxes at each table rather than chasing each other around. The staff is quite attentive though, and will introduce you to a cat/dog or two. In addition to cafe tables there are also a couple of comfortable sofa areas where you can stretch out.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Kimberly Fortuno

Opening hours: Everyday from 6am to 9pm.
